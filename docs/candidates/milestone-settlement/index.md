# Milestone Settlement

## Peers

- vendor
- client
- QA or reviewer

## Base Facts

- vendor delivers artifacts and completion claims
- QA attests test results
- client accepts, rejects, or disputes

## Shared Claim

- `milestone M is payable`

## Why It Is Strong

- more than one peer owns necessary facts
- negative claims matter, such as "not disputed"
- disputes are common
- certification facts are natural, such as client acceptance or auditor signoff

## Certification Ladder

One useful ladder is:

- delivery facts
- QA facts
- client acceptance or exclusion of dispute
- certifier publishes `payable(M)` under a named policy
